About Total Tourism

Welcome to Total Tourism — your premier source for curated travel experiences worldwide. We are committed to delivering reliable, high-quality journeys that prioritize exceptional customer service and uniquely memorable itineraries tailored to each traveler.

Established in 2025 by four college students—Rahdyce, David, Mary, and Timi—Total Tourism began as a project for a Human Computer Interaction course. We are pleased to present an initial prototype designed to explore innovative solutions that make travel planning more efficient and more enjoyable for our users.
